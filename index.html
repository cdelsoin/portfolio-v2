Hey, I'm Cliff

Web Developer

Get to know me!

I’m just a guy who was interested in code since I watched the movie The Matrix as a kid. I decided to turn my interest into a career by enrolling into General Assembly’s Web Dev Immersive program. Building on the skills already learned working in Pharmacology, GA provided me with the tools needed to learn multiple technologies like JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, and Ember. I may not be able to code myself a new wardrobe and car or dodge bullets but building web apps is just as satisfying!

When I'm not cranking out some code you may find me playing out my blues on my guitar, working on my motorcycles, or looking for a new adventure. I love new experiences and adventures! Whether that may be travelling to new city or going hiking and kayaking with friends.
